This is Richfield Connect!

Richfield Connect is a social platform designed to enhance digital engagement. This intends to create an institution-focused online environment where students engage and network building a strong student community.

What does Richfield Connect Do?

  • Feed: Keeps you posted with the latest news and posts from your connections and from the campus.
  • Profile: Create and personalize your profile to your liking.
  • Networking: Connect with fellow students, share ideas, and collaborate on projects.
  • Like: Like content and posts that interest you or that you find relatable.
